Who am I?


My name is Nirek Mehta. I am a student at Babson College. The following page has been developpeed OIM Webtech class, taught by Prof. Gordon. I began diving in the summer of 2019. Ever since my first dive in the swimming pool, I could not wait to dive in the ocean. I am a certified advanced diver and can dive down to 40 meters/130 ft. I like swimming in warm waters that have an abundance of wildife. My personal favorite dive was my first wreck dive in Thailand. I generally go diving with my father.
