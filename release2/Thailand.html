Thailand, country located in the centre of mainland Southeast Asia. Located wholly within the tropics, Thailand encompasses diverse ecosystems, including the hilly forested areas of the northern frontier, the fertile rice fields of the central plains, the broad plateau of the northeast, and the rugged coasts along the narrow southern peninsula. With over 1,000km of coastline Phuket offers sites like extensive reefs, massice drop-offs, ship and airplane wrecks, caves and tunnels. Diving in Phuket is a paradise for scuba divers of all level. Scuba Diving in Phuket is considered to be a world-class diving destination and a paradise for scuba divers. In Phuket, the most frequently visited sites are Racha Noi and Racha Yai. The Thai waters offer divers great opportunity to dive with manta rays, whale sharks, Hawksbill turtles, seahorses and much more.
