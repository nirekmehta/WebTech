What is this site about?

This website is the perfect introduction for first time divers. The website is divided into potential locations that beginner divers could visit. The website also has a page on the diving equipment that gets used and how to make sure that the equipment is safe to use. The website also has interesting facts about scuba diving which is available to peruse for some light reading. I have personally dived at three out of the four sites and can ensure that these locations are worth diving in. My favorite dive site has been Phuket and I have uploaded some personal footage from my dive.
