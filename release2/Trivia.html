Fun Facts (to make point vanish double click text)

  1. The word "SCUBA" is an acronym. It stands for ‘Self Contained Underwater Breathing Apparatus’
  2. Scuba Divers never hold their breath. Holding the breath will cause the diver to ascend therefore, divers must ensure regular airflow.
  3. Water absorbs light. Therefore, when you dive below 10 metres depth, you can’t see red or yellow!
  4. Sound travels five times faster underwater making it impossible to detect the origin of the sound.
  5. Nitrogen narcosis affects all divers, causing them to be feel slightly drunk post dive.
  6. The Great Barrier Reef is the largest living organism on the planet.
  7. We have explored less than 10% of the world’s ocean floor.
  8. Even though Scuba Diving is considered as a risk sport, according to DAN, the fatality happens only once every 211864 dives.
  9. The air SCUBA divers breathe is actually a mixture of compressed gases. Typically, 21% oxygen and 79% nitrogen.
  10. Scuba Diving is a great way to stay fit because on average a diver burns around 600 kilo-calories per hour in temperate waters.
