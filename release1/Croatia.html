Croatia, country located in the northwestern part of the Balkan Peninsula. It is a small yet highly geographically diverse crescent-shaped country. Its capital is Zagreb, located in the north while the famous city of Dubrovnik is in the South. It is extremely attractive for divers because of the numerous dive sites along with the fact that it is the cheapest and, arguably, one of the most beautiful European travel destination. Croatia offers around 1000 islands all adorned with reefs, underwater caverns, pillars and canyons. There are plenty of wrecks to explore as well. Croatia has a plethora of caves where sunlight filters in, making for ethereal seascapes. The country is literally encircled with dive sites and offers divers the opportunity to not just dive in the Adriatic Sea but also to dive in Rivers, Lakes, and sometimes try Ice diving. Divers can practice free diving and explore the numerous above and under water caves.
