Dive Conditions

Water Type: Salty and clear water with temperatures ranging from 70 to 100°F (21 to 38 °C).
Equipment Needed:
  • Masks
  • Fins
  • Lightweight Wetsuit
  • Snorkels
  • Fins
  • GPS and Dive Computer
Observable Marine Life:
  • Marine Mammals (whales, dolphins, dugong, seals)
  • Birds
  • Marine Turtles
  • Sharks and Rays
  • Fish
  • Molluscs
  • Hard Corals
  • Sea anemones
Important Advice: Make sure to not make sudden movements that may affect or break the fragile ecosystem.
