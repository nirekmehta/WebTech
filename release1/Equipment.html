Hover Over Each Apparatus for More Details!

  1. Scuba Mask
    • Visual Inspection. Check for minor cracks that may cause flooding of the mask.
  2. Flippers
    • Visual Inspection. Check for minor cracks in the fins and the straps of the fins.
  3. Snorkel
    • Visual Inspection. Check for minor punctures that may cause flooding of the Snorkel.
  4. Regulator
    • Breathe Test and Visual Test. Try inhaling and check the SPG. Inspect for any tears in the equipment.
  5. Buoyancy Control Device
    • Sound Test. Fill the device and listen to identify if air is leaking from it.
  6. Tanks
    • Visual Test. Look for any erosion and rust formation around the tank and ensure to verify the expiry date.
  7. Wet and Dry Suits
    • Visual Test. Closely examine the suit's neck, cuffs and seams for any rips or slits.
  8. Dive Computers
    • Recommend to soak in unsalinated water at the earliest. Ensure that the computers are serviced once a year.
