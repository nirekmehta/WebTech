Dive Conditions

Water Type: Mild to strong current in the water with temperatures ranging from 13-26°C (57-79°F).
Equipment Needed:
  • Masks
  • Fins with Boots
  • Wetsuits with hood
  • Snorkels
  • Fins
  • GPS and Dive Computer
Observable Marine Life:
  • Cardinalfish
  • Lobster
  • Tubeworms
  • Barracuda
  • Red Coral
  • Sea Stars
Important Advice: Make sure to not make sudden movements that may affect or break the fragile ecosystem.
