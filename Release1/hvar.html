Dive Conditions

Water Type: Salty Water with temperatures between 64.4 to 77°F (25 to 18 °C)
Equipment Needed:
  • Masks
  • Fins with Boots
  • Wetsuit with Hoods
  • Snorkels
  • Fins
  • GPS and Dive Computer
Observable Marine Life:
  • Damselfish
  • Painted Comber
  • Sea Star
  • Cow Bream
  • Peacock Worm
  • Seahorse
Important Advice: Make sure to not make sudden movements that may affect or break the fragile ecosystem.
